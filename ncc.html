National Cadet Corps(NCC) - Our College

Spreading Awareness, Saving Lives

About NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the youth wing of the Indian Armed Forces, aiming to develop leadership, discipline, and a sense of patriotism among students. Our college proudly participates in the NCC program, where cadets undergo training and participate in various activities to prepare them for future leadership roles.

Our Activities

  • Drill Practices
  • Annual Training Camps (ATC)
  • National Integration Camps (NIC)
  • Republic Day and Independence Day Parades
  • Social Service Activities

Impact of NCC

Our NCC unit has been a platform for many students to learn essential life skills like discipline, teamwork, and self-confidence. The training programs also promote physical fitness, mental resilience, and social responsibility, making cadets ready for challenges both in academic and professional life.

For more information, contact the NCC office at ncc@college.com
