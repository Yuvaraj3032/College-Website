About YRC

The Youth Red Cross (YRC) is an organization that promotes humanitarian values, health awareness, and social responsibility. Our college's YRC chapter aims to empower students to contribute positively to society.

Our Initiatives

  • Blood Donation Camps
  • Health Awareness Programs
  • Disaster Relief Assistance
  • Environmental Awareness Campaigns

For more information, contact the yRC office at ncc@college.com
